Alu kan (Fried Potato) Recipie

Description:

This is a Mizo dish called Alu Kan

Ingredients:

  • Alu (potato)
  • Fool oil
  • Salt
  • Onion

Steps:

  1. Chop the alu (potato) into small small cubes
  2. Immerse the chopped alu-s inside water
  3. Turn on the stove and heat up the bel (utensil)
  4. Once the bel heats up nicely, pour in food oil (it does not have to be too much)
  5. Once the oil reaches its vaporising point, chop the onion in half and immerse it in the oil and take it out after 5 seconds.
  6. Now drain the water from the alu-s and put them in the bel
  7. Stir the mixture every 5 to 7 minutes until the alu-s become soft and edible
  8. Then you have just made the Mizo Alu Kan
