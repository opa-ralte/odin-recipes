Bai (a Mizo dish) Recipie

Description:

This is a Mizo Ratuai Bai

Ingredients:

  • Rice (Mizo buh)
  • Food soda
  • Salt
  • Sa-um (animal oil)
  • Leafy vegtables like maian

Steps:

  1. Boil water until it reaches boiling point.
  2. Add 1/2 cup of rice
  3. As the rice begins to get softer, add salt and sa-um and the leafy vegtables
  4. Put the stove in medium heat
  5. Stir the mixture slowly so as to not crush everthing
  6. Wait for around 2 mintues and you are done
