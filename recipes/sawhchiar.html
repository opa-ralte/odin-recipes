Sawhchiar (a Mizo dish) Recipie

Description:

This is a Mizo Sawhchiar

Ingredients:

  • Buh (rice)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Edible oil

Steps:

  1. Boil water until it reaches boiling point.
  2. Add 2/3 cup of rice
  3. As the rice begins to get softer, add salt
  4. Put the stove in medium heat
  5. Add a little bit of oil and stir
  6. Wait until the mixture becomes soft and consistent
  7. Now you can add any toppings you like
  8. Then, you are done.
